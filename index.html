Yixuan Tang

I am a post-doctoral research fellow at the School of Computing, National University of Singapore (NUS), working with Prof. Anthony K. H. Tung at Centre for Trusted Internet and Community (CTIC), and Prof. Wynne Hsu and Prof. Mong Li Lee at Institute of Data Science (IDS). I received my B.Comp. in 2016 and Ph.D. in 2024, from the School of Computing, NUS. My Ph.D. thesis, co-supervised by Prof. Anthony K. H. Tung and Prof. Ng Hwee Tou, focuses on novel approaches and evaluation to enhance the robustness of machine reading comprehension models. My research interests include multi-hop reasoning, misinformation detection and trustworthy AI in healthcare.
